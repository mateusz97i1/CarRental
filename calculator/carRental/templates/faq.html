Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I make a reservation?

A: Making a reservation is quick and easy! Simply visit our website, select your desired car, choose the rental dates, and proceed to the checkout. You can also give us a call, and our friendly customer support team will assist you with the booking process.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. You can also choose to pay with cash, but please note that a valid credit card is still required for security purposes.

Q: Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

A: Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation. To make changes, log in to your account or contact our customer support team. Please check our cancellation policy for any applicable charges based on the modification or cancellation timing.

Q: Is there a minimum age requirement for renting a car?

A: To rent a car with us, you must be at least 21 years old and have a valid driver's license. However, certain car models may have a minimum age requirement of 25. Additional fees may apply for drivers under 25.

Q: Do you offer insurance coverage for the rental cars?

A: Yes, we provide insurance coverage options for our rental cars. Our standard package includes basic coverage, and you can opt for additional coverage for enhanced protection during your rental period.

Q: Can I add an additional driver to the rental agreement?

A: Yes, you can add an additional driver to your rental agreement. Both the primary driver and the additional driver must meet our age and license requirements and will be subject to a nominal fee.

Q: What if I return the car late?

A: We understand that plans can change. If you anticipate returning the car later than the agreed-upon drop-off time, please inform us as soon as possible. Late returns may be subject to additional charges based on the delay.

Q: Are there any mileage restrictions on the rental cars?

A: Our rental cars come with unlimited mileage for most bookings. However, certain promotions or specialty vehicles may have mileage restrictions. Be sure to check the terms and conditions of your reservation.

Q: Can I rent a car for a one-way trip?

A: Yes, one-way rentals are available, allowing you to pick up the car at one location and return it to another. Please check for availability and any associated fees for one-way rentals.

Q: What do I do in case of an accident or breakdown?

A: In the event of an accident or breakdown, please contact our 24/7 emergency support line immediately. We will guide you through the necessary steps and arrange for assistance as needed.
